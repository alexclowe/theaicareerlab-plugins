# Chiropractor Plugin for Claude

Chart SOAP notes, draft medical necessity letters, and generate practice marketing content.

Built by [The AI Career Lab](https://theaicareerlab.com/professions/chiropractor) — AI tools, guides, and weekly digests designed specifically for chiropractors.

## Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/soap-note` | Draft chiropractic SOAP notes with postural analysis, orthopedic testing, adjustment documentation, and insurance-defensible formatting |
| `/medical-necessity` | Generate medical necessity letters citing chiropractic clinical guidelines for insurance authorization and appeals |
| `/pi-narrative` | Draft personal injury and auto accident clinical narratives with mechanism of injury, treatment timeline, and causation analysis |
| `/practice-marketing` | Generate professional practice marketing content including social media posts, blog outlines, newsletters, and Google Business posts |

## Skills

This plugin includes skills that activate automatically when you're working on chiropractic-related tasks:

- **Chiropractic Clinical** — Musculoskeletal assessment, spinal biomechanics, adjustment technique terminology, diagnostic imaging, ICD-10 coding, and evidence-based chiropractic guidelines
- **Practice Growth** — Content marketing, patient retention, review generation, community engagement, and healthcare advertising compliance (HIPAA, FTC)

## Usage examples

```
/soap-note
Visit 8 for 42yo male, chronic low back pain with L radiculopathy. Reports
pain decreased from 7/10 to 4/10 since starting care. Still limited sitting
>30 min for desk job. Exam: lumbar flexion 60% normal, extension 50% normal.
Kemp's positive L. SLR positive at 50 degrees L. L4-5 fixation on palpation,
paraspinal hypertonicity. Treated with Diversified adjustment L4-5, L5-S1,
SI joint. Flexion-distraction L4-5 x 10 min. E-stim lumbar 15 min.
```

```
/medical-necessity
Need authorization for 12 more visits. Patient: 58yo female, cervicogenic
headaches and neck pain after rear-end MVA 6 weeks ago. Initial: headaches
daily, cervical ROM 40% of normal, NDI 64%. Current: headaches 3x/week,
cervical ROM 65% of normal, NDI 42%. Positive Spurling's R. MRI shows
C5-6 disc bulge. Insurer: Blue Cross. Still unable to return to full-time
work as a dental assistant (overhead reaching and sustained postures limited).
```

```
/pi-narrative
MVA case. Patient: 35yo female, rear-ended at stoplight. Estimated impact
30 mph. Wearing seatbelt, airbag deployed. Presented to ER same day — X-rays
negative for fracture, diagnosed with cervical and lumbar sprain/strain.
First chiro visit 3 days post-accident. Initial exam: cervical flexion 30 deg,
extension 20 deg. Positive Spurling's bilat. Pain 8/10 neck, 7/10 low back.
Treated 3x/week x 4 weeks then 2x/week x 8 weeks. Currently at visit 28.
Pain now 3/10 neck, 2/10 low back. ROM 80% of normal. Approaching MMI.
```

```
/practice-marketing
Create 5 social media posts for March: 1 educational post about sciatica,
1 myth-busting post about chiropractic and kids, 1 ergonomic tip for desk
workers, 1 seasonal post about spring gardening injury prevention, and
1 Google Business post promoting our new patient special. Keep tone
professional but friendly. Include hashtag suggestions. Our practice name
is "Summit Spine & Wellness" in Denver, CO.
```

## Disclaimer

All clinical content generated by this plugin is for informational and drafting purposes only. It does not constitute medical or chiropractic advice. The chiropractor is responsible for verifying all clinical information and exercising independent professional judgment before clinical use. Marketing content should be reviewed for compliance with state licensing board rules, HIPAA, and FTC regulations before publishing.
